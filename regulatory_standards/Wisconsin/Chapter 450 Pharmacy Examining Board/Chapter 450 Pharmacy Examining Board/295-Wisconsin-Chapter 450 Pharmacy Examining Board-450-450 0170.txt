---------- ELEMENTS ----------
1. 450.0170.1 (1)
   Each member of the board shall investigate and institute actions for
violations of this chapter by any person and for violation of ch. 961 by
pharmacists. The district attorney of the proper county shall promptly prosecute
any such violation upon notice from any source
